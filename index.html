OUR STORY

FORTY YEARS.

Built in Sitra. Delivered across Bahrain.

Arabesque Carpentry has spent four decades turning technical woodwork into finished spaces of presence: doors, fit-outs, staircases, wardrobes, kitchens, and specialist joinery for civic, religious, retail, and residential projects.

SITRA WORKSHOP
Architectural woodwork with precision finishing, engineered detailing, and large-scale delivery.
WORKSHOP ETHOS

The factory in Sitra is built to handle both repetition and refinement: production-grade capacity for major packages, with enough control to protect the detailing, proportions, and finishing that high-visibility interiors demand.

DELIVERY
